Firefighter Response Analysis

Firefighter Response Frequency

Created a line graph to represent the number of times the Fire Department responded to a 911 call over the timespan of the data set. To the right of the Firefighter analysis is a line graph displaying the Overall 911 Call Frequency. After examination, Firefighter reponses share one common spike to the overall frequency of 911 calls. This could correlate to the spike in the Overal 911 call volume for Montgomery County, PA. The spike could be a result of a historic blizzard that passed through Montgomery County, PA between Jan 22, 2016 & Jan 24, 2016, and according to the NWS (National Weather Service) dumped a record breaking 32"-36" inches of snow. The other spikes in call volume would need additional investigation to determine the cause of the spike in 911 call volume.
